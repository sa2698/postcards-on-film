About

A little context behind the photographs.

Hi! I’m Sri. Postcards on Film is an archive of places and moments I didn’t want to lose.

I’m drawn to light, texture, and the quiet stories that sit in ordinary scenes. I held my first camera at 18 and have dabbled in various forms of photography since. Through this website, I hope to share my favourite moments with you.

Growing up in India, I was always surrounded by an intense tapestry of colour, and that continues to draw me to man uilding my collection of sights from my time here. Hope you enjoy!

If you’d like to collaborate, ask about prints, or just say hello, head to the contact page.
